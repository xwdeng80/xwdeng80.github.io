Xinwei Deng is a Professor of Statistics and Data Science Faculty Fellow at Virginia Tech. He is also a co-director of VT Statistics and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (VT-SAIL). Deng obtained his Ph.D. degree from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2009 under the supervision of Jeff Wu and Ming Yuan. He joined in the statistics department at Virginia Tech since 2011.
Here are his Curriculum Vitae and a summary of his research projects.

Reseaerch Interests

Interface between experimental design and machine learning

Uncertainty quantification and digital twin

Model and analysis of high-dimensional data

Covariance matrix estimation and its applications

Design and analysis of computer experiments

Statistical methods for Nano and emerging technology
