Xinwei Deng is Associate Profoessor of Statistics at Virginia Tech. Deng obtained his Ph.D. degree from Georgia Institute of Techonology in 2009 under the supervison of Jeff Wu and Ming Yuan. He joined in statistics department at Virginia Tech since 2011.
