VT Statistics and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (VT-SAIL)


Artificial intelligence (AI) is data-driven in its core. Establishing a rigorous framework for data collection, model training, uncertainty quantification, and safety analysis is critical for AI. Classical statistical concepts, such as population, representativeness of training data, robustness of the learning, and reliability of the algorithm are all very important to ensure AI to be accurate, robust, and safe. The VT-SAIL lab will focus on methodology development of statistics-guided artificial intelligence, especially on uncertainty quantification, safety analysis, and experimental design thinking for AI.
